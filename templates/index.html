Welcome to the Deployment Project

This project allows you to deploy your applications in various ways,
supporting both Python-based and MERN (MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js) based projects:

  • Traditional Deployment: Deploy your Python-based projects using conventional methods.
  • Amazon EKS Deployment: Utilize Amazon Elastic Kubernetes Service (EKS)
    for advanced deployment for Python-based projects.
  • MERN Stack Deployment: Deploy your MERN (MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js)
    based projects with ease.

Steps:

  1. Select your preferred deployment method.
  2. Follow the instructions for the chosen deployment approach.
  3. Review any messages or feedback provided during the deployment process
  4. Proceed to the next steps or navigate to additional options.

Details:

For more information about the deployment process and configuration,
please refer to the documentation specific to your chosen technology stack (Python or MERN).
